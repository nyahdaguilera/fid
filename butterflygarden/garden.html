Welcome!

Welcome to Fluttering Meadows, a place that is home to a variety of butterflies, as well as beautiful plants. As you stroll through our vibrant garden, you'll be surrounded by a symphony of color, with butterflies gently fluttering from bloom to bloom. Our garden is a celebration of the beauty and grace of these winged creatures, offering a place of tranquility and connection to the natural world.
