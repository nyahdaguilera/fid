Volunteering

Thank you for your interest in volunteering at Fluttering Meadows. Our normal volunteer days are Wednesday mornings and the first two (2) Saturdays of each month, we reach to volunteers when we have additional opportunities. We take pride in our all-volunteer Board of Directors who make everything possible. Who can volunteer? Organizations – Please use the Contact Us page to provide your information. Large and Small Groups – Please use the Contact Us page to provide your information. Individuals – complete form below. Students [accredited community service hours offered] – complete form below. Where to volunteer? Gardens There is always work to be done around the gardens such as pulling weeds, clearing brush, planting new plants, raking, landscaping, etc. Individuals interested in volunteering with the gardens are welcome to attend any open work day on 1st and 2nd Saturday of the month. Historic Home Home volunteers are needed on a per project basis and must be scheduled in advance with our staff. Carpentry Cleaning Painting Masonry General Maintenance Construction Administration We are always seeking to grow our volunteers in roles of organization, administration, planning and marketing.
